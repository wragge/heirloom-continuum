The Heirloom Dominion was proclaimed by the to be the human race’s greatest achievement, at least within the scope of recent memory. The space station boasts a colossal size of 8576 metres long and a weight of approximately 56000 tons. It sits on the outskirts of solar system, barely within our sun’s gravitational pull. It was built to be not only a research station but also as a military base and as a launch station. You are a young cadet on this space station, training to be part of the military forces that will one day go to conquer space beyond our solar system and the Heirloom Dominion. However maybe all at The Heirloom Dominion is not what it seems, there have always been conspiracies about top secret government experiments being performed on the space station, so what will happen when you get caught right in the middle of it?
